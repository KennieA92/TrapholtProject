Arne Jacobsen Kubeflex

Permanent Exhibition

The house is called Kubeflex, as it consists of cube-shaped modules that can be flexibly assembled in countless ways and resized as needed.

Arne Jacobsen designed Kubeflex in 1969-70, the year before he died in 1971, but it was never put into production. Until 2002, the Jacobsen family used the house as a summer home, and since 2005 it has belonged to Trapholt. The house is the only one of its kind and is decorated with Arne Jacobsen design
